"The Agile Manifesto": we can't plan for everything, so let's make good, working software with what we know.

Hard truths of programming

  • You're bad at estimating time. (So am I.)
  • You're bad at focusing on long, large tasks. (So am I.)
  • You're bad at patiently waiting to see results. (So am I.)

...so what do we do?

Why agile?

  • To mitigate all that bad stuff, we want to start small, make small changes, and build piece-by-piece.
  • Multiple flavors of agile to achieve this: Scrum and Kanban most common
  • It's also a serious buzzword in tech!

    • Agile isn’t how fast you can go, it’s how fast you can turn.

    Features and user stories

    As a ___ I want ___ So that ___

    • Features are written as epics and distilled into user stories
    • Epics are your 30,000 ft view; stories are how you get there
    • Epics & stories kept in your "backlog"
    • Is your feature "significant"?

    Let's talk Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

    • It doesn't have to be pretty: focus on usable + useful
    • Cheaper to build a few important features then test the market!
    • Meant to be iterated on - if users buy it, you need to keep selling!
    • Needs to be scoped similar to user stories: "right-sized"

    Activity: User story ideation

    A car MVP?

    • Again, the Model T
      • As a COMMUTER I want A MECHANICAL TRANSPORTATION OPTION so that I DON'T HAVE TO FEED A HORSE
      • As a DRIVER I want A WHEEL TO TURN MY CAR so that I HAVE AN EASY AND UNDERSTANDABLE WAY TO MOVE LEFT OR RIGHT
      • As a DRIVER I want A PEDAL THAT MAKES MY CAR MOVE so that I CAN ACCELERATE WITHOUT TAKING MY HANDS OFF THE TURNING WHEEL
      • As a DRIVER I want A MIRROR THAT REFLECTS BEHIND ME so that I CAN SEE ANY POTENTIAL HAZARDS BEHIND ME

    Activity: User story ideation

    A drying machine MVP?

    • Kind of a trick question: it's a clothesline!
      • As a PERSON WHO OWNS CLOTHES I want A PLACE TO PUT THEM WHERE THEY DRY so that I HAVE SOMEWHERE TO PUT THEM AFTER I HAVE FINISHED WASHING THEM
      • As a HOMEOWNER I want MY WET CLOTHES TO NOT DRIP IN THE HOUSE so that I DO NOT HAVE WATER DAMAGE OR MOLD OR MESS
      • As a CLOTHES WASHER I want MY WET CLOTHES TO DRY QUICKLY so that I CAN WEAR THEM AGAIN SOON
      • Bonus! As a CLOTHES OWNER I want MY CLOTHES TO DRY INSIDE THE HOUSE so that RAIN WILL NOT RUIN THEM and so that MY NEIGHBORS WON'T STEAL THEM
