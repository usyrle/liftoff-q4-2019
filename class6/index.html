Software Interviews

  • Live Coding - solve a problem real-time
  • Technical - talk your technical knowledge
  • Behavioral - see what you're like as an employee

What's the Interview Process Like?

  1. Phone Interview (sometimes)
    • Usually, just screening
  2. In-Person Interview
    • Can be live coding, technical, behavioral
    • Sometimes two parts - do a technical, come back for behavioral
    • ...or do behavioral, come back for whiteboarding
  3. Follow-ups as needed

General Interview Tips

  • Dress professionally!
  • Learn about the company
  • Bring a notebook
  • Take your time answering, paced is always better than rushed

Interview Tips Continued

  • Think about your 5-year plan
  • Ask them questions:
    • What team will I be on / what will I work on?
    • What learning opportunities are there, e.g. training?
    • What are the expectations? Work hours, on-call, etc.
    • When do you think you'll have a decision?
