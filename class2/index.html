Flavors of Agile

Scrum:

  • Product Owner, Scrum Master, Dev Team
  • Two-week "sprints"
  • Iteration + improvement
  • Release every sprint!

Kanban:

  • Flexible team def
  • Limited WIP
  • Continous backlog grooming
  • Release when you're ready!

Above all: try, pick, and improve!
