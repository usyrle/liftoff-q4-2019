User stories

Rome wasn't built in a day and your app won't be either

  • Features are written as epics and distilled into user stories
  • Epics are your 30,000 ft view; stories are how you get there
  • As a ___ I want ___ So that ___
  • Don't make any one blank too big!

Further reading: Agile Epics

Further reading: Agile User Stories

  • Epics & stories kept in your "backlog"
  • Prioritize aggressively! Think:
    • What's most useful to the user?
    • How hard will each feature be to implement?
    • Does this feature depend on others? Vice versa?
